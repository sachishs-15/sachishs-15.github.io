Research

My interest lies broadly in the domain of Robot Learning, particularly at the juncture where it intersects with Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. Through the integration of Reinforcement Learning with the capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs), there's potential to revolutionize how robots perceive their surroundings and perform tasks, leading to enhanced learning.

I want to see a world where robots can interact safely with humans while continually learning and improving their behavior. Automation of everyday tasks, self-driving cars, these are the kind of things that intrigue me a lot! So I wish for these "straight out of a fiction book" scenarios to manifest in reality.
